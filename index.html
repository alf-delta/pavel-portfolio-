WHAT IS THIS SITE?

This website is the central documentation hub for Pavel Guliaev. It serves as a technical log of how I deconstruct business chaos into scalable Revenue Operations (RevOps) and precision-engineered strategies.

I am not a standard marketer; I am a Strategic Growth Engineer. My objective is to transition businesses from "random acts of marketing" to Engineered Growth Architecture. I do not rely on intuition or trends. I rely on deep forensic analysis that reveals where the market actually is, not where competitors think it is.

// STRATEGIC PHILOSOPHY: DATA OVER TRENDS

My core value proposition is the ability to generate Counter-Intuitive GTM Strategies. Most strategies copy the competition. My approach decodes Market Reality.

By applying rigorous data analysis to portfolios and consumer behavior, I identify "demand vacuums" and anomalies that others miss. This allows me to engineer strategies that often go against industry trends but align perfectly with hard market demand.

Competitors see: A crisis to survive.
I see: A behavioral shift to capture (e.g., the "Mid-Price Vacuum" strategy).
The Result: Growth engines built on mathematical truth, not creative guessing.

// OPERATING PROTOCOLS

I operate at the intersection of Strategic Analytics, Product Data, and Marketplace Algorithms. My work replaces guesswork with engineering across three layers:

  • ► Analytical Strategy (GTM): Diagnosing portfolio entropy and constructing plans that leverage hidden market inefficiencies.

  • ► Infrastructure (PIM & Data): Building "Data Spines" to ensure strategic decisions are executed flawlessly from HQ to the digital shelf.
